Packages

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Best For: History, forts, royal architecture, cultural shopping

Ideal Trip Duration: 2 to 3 days

Day 1:

  • Visit Amber Fort (do the light and sound show if possible)
  • Stop at Jal Mahal for photos
  • Explore Hawa Mahal and City Palace
  • Night: Shop at Johari Bazaar for handicrafts and jewelry

Day 2:

  • Visit Jantar Mantar
  • Explore Nahargarh Fort (great sunset viewpoint)
  • Try traditional lunch at Chokhi Dhani

Day 3:

  • Visit Albert Hall Museum and Galta Ji (Monkey Temple)
Category Estimated Cost (₹) Notes
Transportation 3,000 Train/bus + local cabs/auto
Accommodation 3,500 2 nights in 3-star hotel
Food 1,500 Rajasthani thali, cafes
Local Guide & Tourism 1,000 Amber Fort, City Palace, entry fees
Room Booking / Misc. 500 Advance booking or souvenirs
Total 9,500

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Best For: Spiritual experience,Ganga river, ancient culture

Ideal Trip Duration: 2 to 3 days

Day 1:

  • Early morning boat ride on Ganga during sunrise
  • Walk along Assi Ghat to Dashashwamedh Ghat
  • visit kashi vishwanth temple

Day 2:

  • explore old city lanes and street food
  • visit sarnath(Buddha preached his first sermon here)
  • attend the evning ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedha ghat

Day 3:

  • Visit handloom workshops for famous banarasi silk sarees
Category Estimated Cost (₹) Notes
Transportation 3,000 Train/flight + local autos
Accommodation 2,500 Ganges-view guesthouse (2 nights)
Food 1,200 Local eateries, street food
Local Guide & Tourism 1,000 Boat ride, temples, heritage walk
Room Booking / Misc. 500 Tips, small purchases
Total 8,200

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Best For: Mountains, rivers, adventure activities

Ideal Trip Duration: 3 to 4 days

Day 1:

  • Explore Mall Road and nearby shops
  • Visit Hidimba Temple and Manu Temple

Day 2:

  • Go to Solang Valley for activities like ropeway, paragliding, skiing (depending on season)

Day 3:

  • Visit Old Manali, cafes, and relax near the river
  • Optional trip to Naggar Castle if interested in photography

Day 4:

  • Trip to Rohtang pass (permit requried , seasonal)
Category Estimated Cost (₹) Notes
Transportation 5,500 Volvo bus or shared cab from Delhi/Chandigarh
Accommodation 5,000 3 nights in a mountain-view stay
Food 2,500 Local dhabas & cafés
Local Guide & Tourism 2,000 Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass permits
Room Booking / Misc. 1,000 Advance, heater charges, etc.
Total 16,000

Kerala (Munnar + Thekkady + Alleppey + Kochi)

Best For: Nature, hill stations, waterfalls, backwaters, relaxation

Best Time October to March

Ideal Trip Duration:5 to 6 Days

Day 1: Kochi → Munnar

  • Scenic 4 hour mountain drive
  • Stop at Cheeyappara Waterfalls
  • Check-in to resort with tea plantation view
  • Calm evening walk

Day 2:Munnar Sightseeing

  • Eravikulam National Park
  • Tea Museum
  • Echo Point, Kundala Lake
  • Top Station view for sunset

Day 3: Munnar → Thekkady

  • Drive to Thekkady
  • Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary boat ride
  • Evening Kalaripayattu / Kathakali show

Day 4: Thekkady → Alleppey

  • Travel to Alleppey
  • Check in Houseboat
  • Enjoy backwater cruise, meals on board, peaceful stay

Day 5: Alleppey → Kochi

  • Travel back to Kochi
  • Explore Fort Kochi, marine drive, cafes
  • Shopping for spices and handicrafts

Day 6 (Optional): Kochi City Tour or Return

  • Lulu Mall / Jew Street / Museums
  • Return journey
Category Estimated Cost (₹) Notes
Transportation 6,500 Flights/train + local cab/bus
Accommodation 8,000 5 nights in resort + 1 night houseboat
Food 3,000 South Indian & seafood dishes
Local Guide & Tourism 3,000 Tea estate tour, backwaters, entry fees
Room Booking / Misc. 1,500 Advance bookings, tips
Total 22,000
